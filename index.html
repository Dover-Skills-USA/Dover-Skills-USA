Programs at a glance

Commercial & Advertising Art

Students learn how to use the elements and principles of design to convey a message and use for commercial projects. These skills allow students to gain important knowledge on how printing and commercial advertisments are made.

This program can prepare students for careers such as Graphic Designer, Printing Press operator, Art Director, and so much more.

Learn More

Engineering

Students create and design innovative solutions to problems using engineering principles and put those solutions to test through may activities. These classes will set students up for success in any problems or situations that they may encounter in the future.

This program can prepare students for careers such as Civil Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, CNC Machine Operator, and so much more.

Learn More

Marketing

Students put their advertising skills to the test through many marketing challenges this program puts them through. They will learn how to use modern techonology and tactics to create meaningful promotions to enhance their business skills for years to come.

This program can prepare students for careers such as Marketing Executive, Sales, Social Media Marketing, and so much more.

Learn More

Providing Career Opportunities

Our CTE programs give students meaningful opportunities they would otherwise be missing. These classes and programs allows students to get a head start in a career that is otherwise seen as unorthodox.

Meet our CTE teachers and the individuals that make these opportunities be possible!

Mission

Our programs seek to prepare students for challenging yet rewarding careers in fields that are not so ordinary. These careers can set them up for other opportunities outside of their respective fields as well. Even if the students do not find a job in these fields, the skills aquired during these programs are benefitial in many other ways.
