Mission

Our programs seek to prepare students for challenging yet rewarding careers in fields that are not so ordinary. These careers can set them up for other opportunities outside of their respective fields as well. Even if the students do not find a job in these fields, the skills aquired during these programs are benefitial in many other ways.
